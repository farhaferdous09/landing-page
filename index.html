FARHA FERDOUS

Hi! I'm an aspiring software engineer studying computer science at City University of New York, Queens College. I'm currently a junior. I'm passionate about the intersection of tech and healthcare, particularly in the fields of ML and bioinformatics. As a board member of my Girls Who Code college loop, I actively engage in promoting tech education and empowering women in tech. My interests lie in leveraging data science to drive innovations in biotech, which I explored in a collaborative AI/machine learning project with Pfizer. I am currently seeking to carve out a niche that seamlessly blends my passion for SWE with my fascination for medical applications of tech. Outside of academics, I'm a native NYer who enjoys being a foodie, K-dramas and anime, K-pop, gacha games, webtoons, and exploring NYC.
